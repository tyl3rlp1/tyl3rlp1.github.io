I've always been drawn to bluegrass because of how real and heartfelt it sounds. The music has a certain honesty—the way the instruments blend, the fast rhythms, and the stories in the lyrics. It reminds me of simpler times and has a way of lifting my mood. I enjoy how each song feels like it was made to be shared, whether on a front porch or at a festival. It’s music that feels right to me.
